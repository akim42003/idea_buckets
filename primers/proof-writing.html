2. Types of Proofs You Actually Use

Direct Proof

Template
Assume hypotheses
Apply definitions + known results
Derive conclusion

Used for: convexity, PSD arguments, norm inequalities.

Proof by Contrapositive

Form
Prove instead of

Used for: uniqueness, identifiability, rank arguments.

Proof by Contradiction

Form
Assume negation of claim
Derive impossibility

Used for: non-existence, impossibility results, lower bounds.

Pattern

Spectral Arguments

Pattern

Rank Arguments

Pattern

Use rank–nullity
