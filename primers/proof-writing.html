Proof Writing Playbook

Generalizable proof patterns with worked examples

This document is a procedural guide to proof writing. It focuses on reusable argument structures, not domain-specific tricks.

For each major pattern:

  • the logical structure is stated,
  • the components are identified,
  • and a complete example proof is given.
